Who We Are

Crafting Luxury Spaces
With Passion

JS Infra Projects is a leading luxury interior design and infrastructure firm based in Mumbai. With over 15 years of experience, we have delivered bespoke residential and commercial spaces for India’s most discerning clientele — including the Jindal family villa, Manoj Modi’s residence, and landmark projects across Mumbai and Pune.

Our philosophy: every space tells a story. We blend timeless design principles with contemporary aesthetics, premium materials, and impeccable execution to create environments that inspire, comfort, and endure.
